About Us

Project EHKO
EKHO is a next-generation vulnerability scanning platform built for simplicity, speed, and security. Designed by cybersecurity students and practitioners, EKHO empowers individuals and organizations to proactively identify and address weaknesses in their digital infrastructure before they can be exploited. In a world where threats evolve faster than ever, we believe cybersecurity shouldn’t be reserved for large enterprises or technical experts. EKHO is committed to making vulnerability scanning accessible, understandable, and actionable for everyone—from small businesses and startups to IT professionals and students. Our platform supports both network and web application scans, giving users insight into potential misconfigurations, open ports, outdated software, and known vulnerabilities—all presented in a clear, easy-to-navigate interface with real-time reporting and guided remediation steps.

Our Mission

At EKHO, our mission is to democratize cybersecurity by putting powerful vulnerability scanning tools into the hands of everyone who needs them. We aim to bridge the gap between complexity and usability, helping users detect threats, protect their systems, and build a more secure digital world—one scan at a time.

We are committed to:

  • Providing intuitive tools that deliver deep insights without the noise.
  • Educating and enabling users to understand and act on vulnerabilities.
  • Continuously evolving our platform to stay ahead of emerging threats.

Whether you're a developer, a security analyst, a student, or a business owner—EKHO is here to help you listen for the threats others don’t hear.
